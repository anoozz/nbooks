questions
=========
* how is attachment of certificates be done?

relationships
=============
* company (as clinic) has information.
* company has customers (patients).
* company can attach certificates and print customer records with it.
* company has items (for selling, mostly services e.g. bracing, tooth canal, etc.)
* company issues invoices (receivables) to customers with credit.
* company enter sales receipts when cash is received.
